# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, collaborative, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and outcomes.
- Assume positive intent and clarify before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this code.
